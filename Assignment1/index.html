About Me

I live to bring imaginative ideas out into the real world: a concept, a design solution, a dream, take it from point A to point B and make sure it lands on C. Effectively and with good taste.

Currently, I channel most of my creative focus into graphic design and becoming a strong front-end web developer. The remaining amount I use to illustrate, and take photos. In the past I've dabbled in music when I played bass in a band, tie-dye (big and complex reactive dye tapestries), lithography, and painting.

I am located in Los Angeles but travel frequently to the Bay Area and to National and State Parks. If you want to connect, send me a message!
